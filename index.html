Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.

You will the game by matching your Total Score to the random Goal you were given at the start. You will lose the game if your Total Score goes above the Goal.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the crystal game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Good Luck!
